Travel Guide: San Francisco

Welcome to "The Best of San Francisco," a Gayot travel guide featuring top restaurants, luxury hotels and spas, must-see attractions and other important information for sophisticated travelers and diners. Whether you're a local or tourist, our "best of" destination guide will steer you to the hippest highlights in San Francisco and the surrounding Bay Area.

The Best of San Francisco



BEWARE "THE ROCK"
Alcatraz Cruises
Often referred to as "The Rock," Alcatraz attracts more than a million visitors each year. The island's somber past as the site of a federal prison — the infamous Al Capone served time there — ignites the curiosity of many visitors. Alcatraz Cruises conducts tours of the abandoned penitentiary, offering early bird excursions for those that want to beat the crowd, as well as night tours for people willing to brave the dark.

Book your tour at the Alcatraz Cruises official website
